# Intermediate Additional Resources

## Moodle Documentation Areas to Review

Use these areas for self-study before and after the course:

- Managing courses and categories.
- Users, cohorts, groups, and enrolment methods.
- Roles, permissions, capabilities, and contexts.
- Site reports, logs, analytics, and completion tracking.
- Plugins, themes, scheduled tasks, cron, backups, and restore.

## Organization-Specific Resources to Collect

Bring or bookmark your local references if they are available:

- Course creation and rollover policies.
- Enrolment ownership matrix.
- Plugin approval process.
- Maintenance window calendar.
- Support escalation paths.
- Data retention and privacy requirements.

## Personal Learning Log

During the course, record:

- Moodle settings you need to review after training.
- Processes that need clearer ownership in your organization.
- Reports you can use for recurring support questions.
- Risks that should be added to your team’s change checklist.
